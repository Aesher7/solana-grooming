Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 2024

Solana Dog Grooming ("we," "us," or "our") respects the privacy of our Colorado customers. This policy explains how we collect and use your data in compliance with the Colorado Privacy Act (CPA).

1. Personal Data We Collect

  • Identifiers: Name, phone number, email address, and physical address provided via our booking form.
  • Pet Information: Dog breed, age, medical history, and grooming preferences.
  • Usage Data: IP address and cookie data used for website analytics.

2. Purposes of Processing

We process your data to fulfill grooming appointments, communicate schedule changes, and improve our website experience. We do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

3. Your Rights under the CPA

As a Colorado resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: You can request a copy of the data we have collected about you.
  • Right to Correction: You may correct inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Right to Deletion: You may request that we delete your personal data.
  • Right to Opt-Out: We do not sell data, but we honor "Universal Opt-Out" signals (like GPC) for any targeted advertising.

4. Contact for Privacy Requests

To exercise your rights, please email us at privacy@solanagrooming.com. We will respond within 45 days.
